Practical Tips, FAQs & Success Stories
Success Story: Sushil Kumar, Haryana

By using drip irrigation under PMKSY and selling wheat via e-NAM, Sushil increased his profit by 30% in 2024. He also received ‚Çπ6,000 support from PM-KISAN and insured his crop under PMFBY.

Tip: Maximize Profits
  • Check mandi prices daily before selling.

  • Use certified seeds and maintain crop rotation.
  • Adopt water-saving irrigation and pest management.
FAQ: How to Apply for PM-KISAN?

Visit pmkisan.gov.in, register with Aadhaar and land details, or contact your local agriculture office.

FAQ: How to Get Soil Health Card?

Submit a soil sample at your nearest KVK or agri office. Get a card with personalized fertilizer advice within 2 weeks.

FAQ: What is the new Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana?

A new flagship scheme (2025) to boost productivity and income in 100 low-productivity districts, with special focus on sustainability and credit access.
