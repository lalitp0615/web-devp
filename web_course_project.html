Central & State Government Schemes for Farmers
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
  • ₹6,000/year direct income support (3 installments)
  • Eligibility: Small & marginal landholding farmers
  • Apply here
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
  • Crop insurance for losses due to natural calamities, pests, diseases
  • Kharif: 2% premium, Rabi: 1.5%
  • Quick claim settlement, direct bank transfer
  • Official site
PM Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)
  • Focus: “Har Khet Ko Pani”, “More Crop Per Drop”
  • Includes: Drip/sprinkler irrigation, watershed, micro-irrigation
  • Details
Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
  • Instant credit up to ₹3 lakh at low interest
  • For crops, dairy, fisheries, allied activities
  • Apply
Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

Subsidies, Support & Mechanization
INTRODUCTION

To empower farmers, reduce input costs, and enhance productivity, the Government of India provides a wide range of subsidies, financial support, and mechanization schemes. These initiatives aim to make modern farming tools, quality inputs, and technology accessible to every farmer—especially small and marginal ones. From seed and fertilizer subsidies to affordable credit, solar pumps, and equipment rental services, these programs are driving inclusive agricultural growth and ensuring better income and sustainability for India’s farming community.


These schemes are implemented through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to ensure transparency and timely delivery of support. Special focus is given to promoting eco-friendly practices, digital agriculture, and women and youth participation in farming. By encouraging the adoption of advanced machinery, agri-drones, precision farming, and solar-powered solutions, the government is transforming traditional agriculture into a modern, resilient, and technology-driven sector.

Practical Tips, FAQs & Success Stories
Success Story: Sushil Kumar, Haryana

By using drip irrigation under PMKSY and selling wheat via e-NAM, Sushil increased his profit by 30% in 2024. He also received ₹6,000 support from PM-KISAN and insured his crop under PMFBY.

Tip: Maximize Profits
  • Check mandi prices daily before selling.
  • Apply fertilizer as per Soil Health Card recommendations.
  • Use certified seeds and maintain crop rotation.
  • Adopt water-saving irrigation and pest management.
FAQ: How to Apply for PM-KISAN?

Visit pmkisan.gov.in, register with Aadhaar and land details, or contact your local agriculture office.

FAQ: How to Get Soil Health Card?

Submit a soil sample at your nearest KVK or agri office. Get a card with personalized fertilizer advice within 2 weeks.

FAQ: What is the new Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana?

A new flagship scheme (2025) to boost productivity and income in 100 low-productivity districts, with special focus on sustainability and credit access.
